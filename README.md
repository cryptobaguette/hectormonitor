## Features

* Multi coins (etc, eth, xmr, ltc, dash)
* Multi pools (ethermine, coinfoundry)
* Get notifications on new payouts
* Get notifications when your miner is down
* Visualize your miner stats
  * Hashrate
  * Active workers
  * Unpaid amount
* Visualize your miner payouts
